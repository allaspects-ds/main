§ I. — On Hospitality

Three quiet disciplines, one unhurried evening.

A short note on how we work — and the small rituals that turn a venue into a memory.

Games

A considered library of indoor and outdoor games — Jenga, croquet, boules, draughts — chosen for their patina and the way they coax strangers into laughter before the speeches.

Decor

From sage organza and hessian runners to candelabras and hurricane vases, our pieces are layered in the manner of an heirloom table, never a showroom.

Personal

Custom belly-bands, hand-poured jams, monogrammed glassware — small printed and pressed objects that read like a love letter from the day itself.
