§ Appendix — On the small print

Terms & Conditions.

A short, plainly-worded set of terms covering hire, delivery, damages and our quiet refund policy.

Hire

All hire is on a standard 3-day basis unless otherwise agreed in writing. Extra days are charged per item, per day, as listed on the relevant page.

Deposit & payment

A non-refundable deposit of 25% is required to reserve a date. The remaining balance is due 14 days before the hire start date.

Damages & loss

The hirer is responsible for the safe return of all items. Lost or damaged items will be charged at their replacement cost.

Delivery & collection

We deliver and collect within Northamptonshire and the surrounding counties. Mileage and labour outside this area are quoted on request.

Cancellation

Cancellations made more than 30 days before the hire start date will receive a refund of any monies paid above the deposit. Cancellations within 30 days will be charged in full.

Personal touches & printed work

Idea pages, belly-bands, jam labels and other personalised items start from £75. Proofs are provided ahead of production; please allow 4–6 weeks for final printing.

Photography

We occasionally photograph our pieces in situ. If you would prefer we did not, please let us know in advance.

For any questions, please email allaspects.ds@gmail.com.
