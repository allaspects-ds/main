About Us

Two people, one shared passion — celebrating life's special moments with people we love, and now with you.

All Aspects is made up of two people, Damian and Suzanne. We have a shared passion for celebrating life's special moments. We have always delighted in spoiling our friends and family, bringing people together in celebration — and here we are, having decided to start a venture into party and event hire and planning.

From our small workroom in Sywell, Northamptonshire, we keep a considered library of indoor and outdoor games, glassware and candlelight, organza and hessian, and the small printed graces that mark a day as yours.

— Damian & Suzanne · MMXIX
